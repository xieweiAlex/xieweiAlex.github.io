Life principles in my early 30s

Alex Xie

April 6, 2020

As as a lifetime learner turned 30, I’ve learned a lot during the self-exploratory journey. I’ve found some principles work for me very well, I’d like to write them down publicly, let the sunlight be the disinfectant.

Embrace English

I can’t emphasis more about the importance of English since I live in the country which English is the official language, I work in the industry English is in dominate position. General English ability (read, speak, write) would be one of my bottleneck for the foreseeable years to come. It takes time, I know that, I hope I can shorten the path.

I’m almost doing everything while learn English, I’ve always have a E-note with me which highest precedence for my E-notes. I notes English quotes, words, phrase, slangs everyday I encountered, from a casual conversation, official/random email, news media, watch movie / TV show, or just purposefully recite English words in this repo, and 2020 is the 4th year since I started doing it, I’m proud of myself.

Embrace digitalization

I strongly believe everything could be digitalized will be digitalized, it’s just a matter of time and we’re just getting started, I’m so excited standing on the edge of the curve, there are so much potential and opportunities ahead. And I’m also very grateful I stumbled into this field accidentally, and I’ve been benefiting a lot, and I will try to get more and give back more if I can. ðŸ˜ƒ

Sunlight is the best disinfectant

Anything worth doing worthy doing publicly.

I believe transparency is the solution for lots of things I don’t understand, as long as they’re transparent, we can hold them accountable, I’m more open to it.

Open source is taking over the software development.

I’m not into bombshells. Burying yourself into a big, grandiose goal and hope you can make it someday and astonish the world, that’s not my style. Just like agile philosophy in software programming, I’d like to just crank it out prototype and try fast iteration, as long as it’s in the right direction, we can keep progressing.

Use tools to make our life better

Of course thinking and theory and more comprehensive and they have bigger impact, tools just like materialized object. I know that, but I’m too lazy to frequently learn new ideas, but I still want to make my life easier in a easily way. At end of the day, using tool is biggest fact we differentiated with animals. I hope it would one of the way leverage my learning ability and changing velocity.

One of my favoriate tool is Vim, I’ve used it for years, I’m still learning it on daily basis, I’m using it on minute basis once I’m on my laptop. I almost only editing/writing everything in Vim environment. This kind of tool has implemented many philosophies and values, learning from a practical tangible tool about those idea is just more fun. ðŸ˜ƒ

Scaling, Scaling, Scaling

I’ve become old enough to don’t care much about many frivolous things including some nonsense traditions. I’d like to do things they can be scaled up, they can be beneficial in a long run.

Be happy and Be nice

Having a happy balanced life style is very crucial in a long run, most of our time is in the journey, I’d like to enjoy the journey, not only the destination.
