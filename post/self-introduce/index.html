Personal traits

I love animals without a doubt, I always following a handful of cute animal public accounts in any media whatever I use, like Instagram, Twitter, FB or Wechat. Sports

Looking back my grow up history, I liked all kinds of sports. Doing exercise makes me healthy and happy in general, and I always get friends while doing sport. I spent a big amount of teenage time playing basketball, I loved it. I played roller skating for years, then years of roller blading in college. I met my first girlfriend while playing roller skate in college. After college, I started like jogging regularly, most of time after at sunset while listening podcast. Recently started in 2019, I started doing weight lifting(or muscle building), skateboarding and riding motorcycle (I consider riding motorcycle is a sport üò∫)

Ambivert

I’m not an introvert person for sure, I can and willing to express my feeling and affection relative easily. I talk a lot when with people or topic I’m interested in, I believe communication is the key üîë for lots of problems. I enjoy exerting my personal influence to make things be good as well. But I don’t have many friends, I’m not a fan of gathering or party, social events drain my energy significantly. I rarely contact any of my friends which I know I should do.

Open-minded

I’m very open-minded and adaptive, I’m a typical liberal in America’s political spectrum, I’m open to a lots of novel ideas, I often got mind blown by those different ideas, and I enjoy exploring them. I despise any nonsense traditions no matter what culture it belongs or how long it has been inherited.

Optimist

I’m a natural optimist, I do feel happy when things are promising, I tend to believe things are going to be good easily. When things are not promising, I tend to make some changes, I always looking for changes, either physically or mentally, consciously or unconsciously, I believe changes bring to good, mistakes better than regrets, etc.

Rationalism

I’m a very rational person. I like doing analysis and make good decisions. I believe our life progress bases on the decisions we make every day, whether it’s big or small. I tend to analysis the world/society around me, I believe things can be scientific tested.

Adventurist

I enjoy the feeling of risk-taking, it makes me feel cool and alive. I’m not a fan of safe bet, that’s just too boring. I enjoy the adrenalin spike while riding motorcycle in open freeway(of course I wear protective gear all the time). I enjoy any of those extreme sports, specially those sports require split-second decision making which spikes the adrenalin release. I do those sports to make myself both mentally and physically being strong, and I feel happy when I’m feeling strong ‚úä.
